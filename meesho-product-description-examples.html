Meesho Listing Copy

Meesho product description examples for sellers.

Meesho copy should be simple, direct, and easy to understand. Focus on product facts, value, use case, and what the buyer receives.

Example: fashion product

Women’s printed cotton kurti for daily wear. Soft breathable fabric, straight fit, round neck, and three-quarter sleeves. Suitable for office, college, casual outings, and home use. Pair with leggings, jeans, or palazzo pants.

Example: home product

Wall-mounted bathroom shelf made for daily storage. Useful for shampoo, soap, skincare bottles, and small bathroom items. Space-saving design keeps the area neat and easy to clean.
