Amazon workflow

  1. Research the primary keyword and long-tail variations.
  2. Create a title that includes product type, variant, pack size, and main benefit.
  3. Write bullets that answer use case, material, compatibility, care, and trust questions.
  4. Add backend keyword ideas that are relevant but not repetitive.
  5. Use reviews to find objections and improve images or FAQs.
