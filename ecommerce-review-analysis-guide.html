Customer Insights

Ecommerce review analysis guide.

Reviews are a live research library. They show what buyers expected, what disappointed them, what language they use, and what your listing should explain better.

Sort review insights into five buckets

Repeated complaintsUnexpected benefitsMissing specificationsBuyer use casesComparison languageKeyword phrases

Use negative reviews carefully

Do not copy competitor review text. Summarize the pattern and fix the buying experience. If buyers complain about size, add scale images and measurements. If they complain about durability, clarify material and usage limits.

Turn reviews into listing updates

  1. Add missing details to bullets and descriptions.
  2. Create image callouts for frequent questions.
  3. Use buyer language in natural keyword sections.
  4. Update FAQs and comparison notes.
  5. Feed the strongest objections into your listing score audit.
