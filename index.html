FAQ

Common questions

Does it work for all product categories?+

Yes. SellSmart AI is trained on millions of marketplace listings across electronics, fashion, home & kitchen, beauty, sports, and more. It adapts its output to your product's category automatically.

How accurate is the multilingual SEO?+

We go beyond word-for-word translation. Our AI localizes keywords based on how native speakers in each region actually search — capturing regional phrases, colloquialisms, and high-intent search patterns that generic translators miss.

Can I generate listings in bulk?+

Bulk generation is available on the Growth plan. You can upload a CSV of product names and get all listings generated simultaneously — ideal for large catalogues or agencies managing multiple seller accounts.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes — every plan comes with a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You'll have full access to all features of your chosen plan during the trial period.

Which marketplaces are supported?+

Currently: Amazon India, Amazon Global, Flipkart, Meesho, Myntra, and Shopify stores. We're actively adding more platforms — Etsy, eBay, and WooCommerce support are on the roadmap for Q3 2026.
