Install safely

  • Official website: www.salesmart.in
  • No APK or third-party download required
  • Works on Android, iPhone, Windows and Mac
  • Your account and credits stay synchronized

Android or desktop Chrome

  1. Open www.salesmart.in in Chrome.
  2. Open the browser menu.
  3. Select Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm the installation.

iPhone or iPad

  1. Open www.salesmart.in in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Select Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add to install the shortcut.

Frequently asked questions

Is there a SaleSmart APK?

No official APK is required. Install the progressive web app directly from www.salesmart.in using your browser.

Is the app free?

You can create an account and start with the available free trial. Additional AI generations use credit packs.

Where is the official login?

Use the official SaleSmart AI account page. Avoid entering your account details on unrelated websites.

Does it work offline?

The installed app can open an offline screen, but AI generation and account synchronization require an internet connection.
