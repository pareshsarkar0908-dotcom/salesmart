Examples

AI product description examples for ecommerce sellers.

Use these examples as patterns. The best AI-generated descriptions still need product facts, proof, and careful editing before publishing.

Fashion example

This cotton straight kurti is made for comfortable daily wear. The breathable fabric, simple print, and easy fit make it suitable for office, college, travel, and casual outings. Pair it with leggings, jeans, or palazzo pants for a clean everyday look.

Home product example

Keep your bathroom shelf organized with a wall-mounted stainless steel rack designed for shampoo, soap, skincare bottles, and daily essentials. The compact design saves floor space and helps keep wet areas easier to clean.

Pet product example

This adjustable dog harness gives medium-sized dogs comfortable chest support during walks. The padded design helps reduce pressure around the neck, while adjustable straps make it easier to get a secure fit.

Before and after pattern

Weak copy says: “High quality product for daily use.” Strong copy says: “A compact stainless steel shelf for storing shampoo, soap, and skincare bottles in small bathrooms. The wall-mounted design saves floor space and keeps daily essentials easy to reach.” The second version is more specific, more useful, and naturally includes search context.

How to adapt examples

  1. Replace the category with your exact product type.
  2. Add material, size, quantity, and variant details.
  3. Add one use case and one trust detail.
  4. Remove claims you cannot prove.
  5. Check the result against the marketplace listing checklist.
