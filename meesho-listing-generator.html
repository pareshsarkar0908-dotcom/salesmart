Meesho listing workflow

  1. Start with simple product facts: material, size, color, quantity, and use case.
  2. Write a plain title that buyers can understand quickly.
  3. Add short benefits instead of long technical paragraphs.
  4. Include what is inside the package and any care note.
  5. Localize the copy when your audience prefers Hindi, Hinglish, or another Indian language.
