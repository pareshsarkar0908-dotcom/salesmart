Amazon India SEO

Amazon product title examples for Indian sellers.

A good Amazon title helps the buyer understand the product before they click. Put the most important product facts early and avoid repeating keywords unnaturally.

Simple title formula

Brand + Product Keyword + Key Feature or Material + Size or Quantity + Use Case or Buyer Benefit.

Examples

Steel Lunch Box for Office, Leak Resistant, 3 ContainersCotton Kurti for Women, Straight Fit, Daily Wear, BlueOrganic Green Tea Bags, 100 Count, No Added SugarDog Harness for Medium Dogs, Adjustable, Padded Chest SupportWall Mounted Bathroom Shelf, Stainless Steel, Rust ResistantKids Water Bottle, BPA Free, School and Travel, 750 ml

What to avoid

Do not stuff the same keyword multiple times, add unsupported claims, hide important variant details, or use vague words like “best” without proof. Buyers and marketplaces both respond better to clear product facts.

Next step

After the title, write bullets that answer the buyer’s real objections: durability, size, material, fit, compatibility, safety, ingredients, care, and return concerns.
