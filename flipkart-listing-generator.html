Flipkart optimization workflow

  1. Write the product title around the clearest product phrase.
  2. Use highlights for specifications buyers compare quickly.
  3. Add a description that explains fit, use, care, or compatibility.
  4. Check reviews and questions for missing details.
  5. Refresh the listing when impressions rise but conversions stay weak.
