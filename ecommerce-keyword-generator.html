Keyword clusters

Primary, long-tail, competitor, backend

Use separate keyword groups for product titles, bullets, backend terms, ad campaigns, and content pages.

Why keyword clusters matter

A single keyword is rarely enough. SaleSmart groups search terms by buyer intent so you can decide what belongs in the title, bullets, backend keywords, and ads.

Best targets

Product name, marketplace name, category phrase, use case, ingredient/material, size, buyer problem, and regional language variations.

How to use generated keywords

Put the primary phrase near the front of the title only when it reads naturally. Use long-tail phrases in bullets and descriptions. Save alternate names and use-case phrases for backend keywords, FAQs, image callouts, and ad campaigns. Do not paste every keyword into every field.
