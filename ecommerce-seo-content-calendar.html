SEO Guides / SEO Content Calendar

Publishing System

Ecommerce SEO content calendar for sellers.

Consistent publishing works when every page has a job. Build content around seller questions, buyer objections, marketplace workflows, and product category decisions.

Weekly publishing mix

One marketplace guideOne product description example pageOne keyword or SEO checklistOne review-led improvement pageOne comparison or template pageOne refresh of an existing page

Prioritize pages by business value

Start with pages that lead naturally into your tools: listing generator, keyword generator, review analysis, listing score, multilingual content, and ROI planner. Traffic that cannot convert should come later.

Measure every month

  1. Submit new URLs through sitemap and Search Console.
  2. Watch impressions before expecting clicks.
  3. Improve titles and descriptions on pages with impressions but low CTR.
  4. Add internal links to pages that start ranking.
  5. Refresh old pages with examples, FAQs, and clearer CTAs.

First 30-day plan

  1. Week 1: publish marketplace listing guides and keyword pages.
  2. Week 2: publish product description examples by category.
  3. Week 3: publish review-analysis and conversion improvement pages.
  4. Week 4: refresh pages that got impressions and add internal links to winners.

How to avoid thin content

Every page should include a clear answer, examples, a checklist, common mistakes, and a next step. If a topic cannot support those sections, combine it with a stronger page instead of publishing a weak standalone article.
